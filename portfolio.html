My Work

Project 1 - Flood Prediction and Management using ML and IoT

This project employs a Random Forest algorithm to predict floods in underpasses based on synthetic rainfall data. It integrates an Arduino Uno with an I2C LCD to display warnings when rainfall exceeds certain threshold. The system features a water level sensor and ultrasonic sensors to monitor and manage water levels. In the event of a flood, servomotors automatically close gates on both sides of the underpass, and a water motor helps collect and discharge water through underground pipelines. This solution enhances safety and efficiency in flood management.

Project 2 - Tourism Management System

Travel and Tourism Management System is a computerized system used to manage the operations related to traveling, booking, sightseeing, and arranging accommodation. The system is designed to help travelers to get the idea about the places they are going to visit, the quality of amenities and services, and the attractions at their destination. The objective of the Travel and Tourism Management System project is to develop a system that automates the processes and activities of a travel and the purpose is to design a system using which one can perform all operations related to traveling.
